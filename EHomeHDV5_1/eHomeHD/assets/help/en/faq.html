Why not bind?

  1. A device can only bind an account,if the device has been another account bound,can be authorized to view.
  2. The device is damaged lead to scan two-dimensional code,you can manually enter the serial number,or the device serial number to generate two-dimensional code scanning.
  3. When you add a device prompts the device is not online,make sure your device is receiving power.

Why not use the Internet?

  1. Check the devices connected to the wireless network is working online.
  2. Check whether the wireless router name and password is changed.

How to reset the device?

In the power case,long press the sys button 10S,when the LED indicator turns red,release,waiting for 500s devices automatically reboot to complete restore factory settings.

How to refresh the list of devices?

Under the list of mobile client devices can be manually refreshed.
